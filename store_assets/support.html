Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play HexGomoku?

HexGomoku is played on a hexagonal grid. Two players take turns placing pieces (black and white). The first player to connect five pieces in a row in any direction along the hexagonal grid wins the game.

Can I play against the computer?

Yes! Select Player vs AI on the home screen to play against the built-in AI opponent.

Can I undo a move?

Yes. Tap the Undo button during gameplay to take back your last move.

How does the Hint feature work?

Tap the Hint button to get a suggested move from the AI. This can help you learn strategy and discover new winning patterns.

Is my data collected?

No. HexGomoku does not collect any personal data. All game data (settings, game progress) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server.
