Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

HexGomoku ("we", "our", or "the app") is a hexagonal board game developed for entertainment purposes. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

HexGomoku does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

  • No personal information is collected

Data Storage

All game data (settings, game progress) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

HexGomoku does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

HexGomoku does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
