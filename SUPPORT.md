# HexGomoku – Support

Thank you for playing HexGomoku! If you need help or have feedback, you're in the right place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I play?
HexGomoku is played on a hexagonal grid. Two players take turns placing pieces. The first player to connect **five pieces in a row** (in any direction along the hexagonal grid) wins the game.

### Can I play against the computer?
Yes! Select **Player vs AI** on the home screen to play against the built-in AI opponent.

### Can I undo a move?
Yes. Tap the **Undo** button during gameplay to take back your last move.

### How does the Hint feature work?
Tap the **Hint** button to get a suggested move from the AI. This can help you learn strategy.

### Is my data collected?
No. HexGomoku does **not** collect any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](store_assets/privacy_policy.html) for details.

## Report a Bug

To report a bug, please include:
1. Your device model and iOS/Android version
2. A description of the issue
3. Steps to reproduce the issue
4. Screenshots if possible

You can report bugs via [email](mailto:hexgomoku.game@gmail.com) or [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/eric-gao-uni/hexgomoku/issues).
